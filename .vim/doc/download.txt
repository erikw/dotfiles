Official Installer
==================

You can find the official eclim installer for on eclim's sourceforge
downloads
(http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=145869) page:

- eclim_version.jar (http://sourceforge.net/project/platformdownload.p
  hp?group_id=145869)


Third Party Packages
====================

As an alternative to the official installer, there are also some
packages maintained by third parties for some popular linux
distributions:

- Arch: aur (eclim) (http://aur.archlinux.org/packages.php?ID=7291),
  aur (eclim-git) (http://aur.archlinux.org/packages.php?ID=33120)
